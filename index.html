Data modeling with python

In this project, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of the impact of the informal economy on Pakistan's GDP from 1991 to 2020. My aim was to provide insights into the macroeconomic structure and offer a basis for adjusting key macroeconomic variables affected by the informal economy. I employed various econometric models, including a simple monetary approach and an autoregressive distributed lag (ARDL) model, to estimate the size of the informal economy and tax evasion. My findings revealed significant relationships between the informal economy, tax burden, and GDP, providing valuable insights into the behavioral and structural growth of Pakistan's informal sector.
